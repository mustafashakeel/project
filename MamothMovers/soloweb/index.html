What is an FAQ?

FAQ means Frequently-Asked Questions, and every industry has them. Today's consumer is savvy and cautious and shoppers have a lot of questions that they want answered before they become buyers. People really appreciate knowing that you are aware of their pre-purchase jitters and concerns. Answering anticipated questions up front puts buyers at ease, smoothing the way for an eventual purchase. Three questions is usually enough.

Where do we get all the stuff that goes into a website?

By 'stuff', you probably mean copy and images and such. Copy is the wording on the site, and we write that for you. Images are pictures and we get those from you or from the manufacturer of your product. If you do not have any digital or usable images, we'll give you a quote for a local photographer so you can have professional images made that you can use forevermore. We'll also need your logo. Don't have a logo? Ask our artist for a quote.

Will we be found on Google?

Getting found on Google is a function of SEO or Search Engine Optimization. We know Google and we've built SEO into every one of our websites so yes, you'll be found on Google. If you are marketing in a larger city or area or are in a very competitive industry and really value being ranked high on Google searches, we'll put you in touch with a professional SEO firm that can help you plan your search engine marketing program.
