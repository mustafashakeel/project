How early do I need to schedule my move?

The earlier you schedule the better chance you have of booking the time slot you require. We are a small company and do book up full at peak times. When you schedule with us you will recieve a onfirmation package and we do not over book. Call 604-767-0944 or email info@mammothmovers.ca

2 ) Is there a minimum charge?

Yes, we do charge a three hour minimum work time plus the travel time on all moves. For example a 2 man move has a three hour minimum charge even if it only took 2 and a half hours to complete it.

3 ) How can I save money?
If you can move mid month you will save money. Be prepared, well packed and organized. Everything is time... are we taking the beds apart or are you? Thats time that could be saved if you are on a budget. Gather used packing supplies instead of buying them. Talk to Angel's Moving for guidance, we can help to ensure you get the best results for your dollar.

4 ) Can I pay you with a check or Interac bank card?

No, we ONLY accept CASH, VISA, or Mastercard. When the move is completed we will settle the invoice and have change and a credit card machine for you to pay. Visa is preferred and we accept MC but DO NOT ACCEPT Amex or Interact or checks..
