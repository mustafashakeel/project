Who Should I Vote
   

    In many elections in the past some political parties have tried to make vote banks of the Muslims, a large number of whom are illiterate, by making pronouncements that invoke the emotions of Muslims, eg making Urdu the second language, closing schools for half day on Fridays for Juma congregational prayers, visiting the tombs of famous Muslim saints, Babri Masjid,  lavish public praises for some Muslim religious leaders etc. After half a century of such tricks the average Muslims are tired of them.
    Before Voting for any Party or a  Candidate Study the following

  1. Learn from History: what they had promised and what they had delivered had they focused on the real issues or Fake and emotional Issues which are irrelevent for the progression and advancement of the Community
  2. Agenda and Policy:  the National and local Agenda in regards with Communalism , Social Welfare and Integration of Muslims in the mainstream
  3. Communalism:  Muslims should ask for a plan for action  to Combat Communalism at all levels the political parties should not just condemn it but should come up with a solution
